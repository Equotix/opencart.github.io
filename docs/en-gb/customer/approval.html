Customer Approvals

If you have enabled the customer approval requirement for your customer group, this is the page where customers awaiting approval will be shown.

Approving or Denying a Customer

On the approval listing page, you can find a list of customers that is pending your verification. Simply click on the approve or deny button beside each customer under the "Action" column to process the customer's account.
