GDPR Approvals

Helping business owners comply with the GDPR law, OpenCart has the GDPR feature built in to enable customers to request for their account information as well as to request for an account deletion.

Approving or Denying a Customer

On the GDPR Approval listing page, you can find a list of customers that has requested for his information. Simply click on the approve or deny button beside each request under the "Action" column to process the customer's request.

Once the request has been approved or denied, an email will be sent to the requested email address with the information.
