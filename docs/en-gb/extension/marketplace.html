Marketplace

The Extensions Marketplace allows you to add features to your OpenCart store easily right from your admin panel. You can purchase new extensions and easily install it from this page. However, you will first need an OpenCart account to set it up before you can start using the Marketplace.

The OpenCart Marketplace in your OpenCart store admin.

Setting Up Marketplace

Follow the instructions below to start using the Marketplace in your OpenCart store.

1. You will need to have an OpenCart account. Create one here if you do not have an account, else login instead.

2. Access the "Your Stores" page in your OpenCart account to add your OpenCart store information to your account.

The OpenCart "Your Stores" page is accessible from your OpenCart account dashboard.

3. Click on "Add Store" and enter your domain information where your store is installed on. For example, if your website URL is www.chocolatechip.com, enter "www.chocolatechip.com" into the field.

Adding your store into your OpenCart account.

4. Once the store has been added, click on the "View" button to retrieve the API information generated. Copy the "API Secret" from your account.

Copying your OpenCart API secret from your OpenCart account.

5. Back at your OpenCart store admin, access the Marketplace page and click on the red button at the top right where it says "Signature hash does not match!".

Add your OpenCart account information to your store.

6. Enter your OpenCart username and the API secret which you've copied earlier at step 4. Save when you are done.

Enter your OpenCart account information to your store in this popup.

You are ready to start using the OpenCart marketplace once the setup is complete.
