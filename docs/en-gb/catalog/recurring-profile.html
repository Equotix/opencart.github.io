Recurring Profiles

Recurring profiles are used to setup recurring payments for your customers. This means that the recurring profile could be a subscription or installment, which requires the customer to pay a fee over a period of time, such as a monthly or annual subscription.

Recurring Profiles Listing Page

You can view all the available recurring profiles in the Recurring Profiles page under the Catalog menu. Once you are ready to create a new recurring profile, you can click on the "Add New" button at the top right of the page.

The page displaying the available recurring profiles.

Creating a Recurring Profile

You will be presented with a page for you to describe and setup your recurring profile. The available fields are explained below.

Name * This is the name of the subscription or payment plan.
Recurring Profile
Price This is the price the customer has to pay for the subscription.
Duration This is the number of times the customer will make a payment. Set to 0 if unlimited. If unlimited, the payment plan will continue until cancelled by you or the customer.
Cycle This is the number that will be applied to your frequency option. For example, if this is "2" and the frequency is "Month", this subscription will bill your customer every 2 months.
Frequency This option allows you to choose how often a customer will make a payment, according to the cycle above.
Status This sets whether the recurring profile is active or inactive.
Trial Profile
Trial Price This is the price the customer has to pay during the trial period.
Trial Duration This is the number of times the customer will make a payment before the trial expires.
Trial Cycle This is the number that will be applied to your trial frequency option. For example, if this is "2" and the frequency is "Month", this trial subscription will bill your customer every 2 months.
Trial Frequency This option allows you to choose how often a customer will make a payment, according to the trial cycle above.
Trial Status This sets whether the trial is active for this recurring profile.
Sort Order This is simply for putting your recurring profiles into a certain order, to make your top picks easier to find.
* Required field

The page for creating a new recurring profile.

Assigning to a Product

Once the recurring profile has been created, you can assign it to a product at Catalog > Products > Recurring tab.
