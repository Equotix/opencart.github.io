Reviews

Product reviews can be submitted and viewed by customer on the product page. A customer can fill up a review form and submit it to be approved for publication on the website.

Reviews Listing Page

All the reviews available on your website will be displayed here. You can also approve, edit and delete the reviews from this page. If you wish to add your own review, you can do so by clicking on the "Add New" button.

The page displaying the reviews for the products.

Creating a Review

The review form has the basic fields to request for the necessary information. The fields are explained below.

Author * This is the name of the person who left the review, which will be displayed.
Product * This is the product this review is linked to. This is an autocomplete field. Type in the name of the product and select it from the dropdown.
Text * This is the review text that will be displayed at the store front.
Rating * This is the rating, out of 5 stars.
Date Added This is the date the review was added.
Status This is the status of the review. New reviews by customers will be disabled by default.
* Required field

The page for the input and editing of the product review.

Once the review has been enabled, it will be displayed on the store front.

If you do not want to enable reviews, you can disable it in System > Settings > Options tab
