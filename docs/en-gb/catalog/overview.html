Catalog

The catalog feature is used to manage your products that are made available to your customers. You can be listing products, adding categories or reviewing the reviews your customers have submitted.

The catalog menu has many sub-features and is as follows:

  • Categories
  • Products
  • Recurring Profiles
  • Filters
  • Attributes
  • Options
  • Manufacturers
  • Downloads
  • Reviews
  • Information

The sections above will be further elaborated in this user guide.
