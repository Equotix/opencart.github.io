Product Recurring Tab

Recurring profiles can be added to the product to make it a recurring subscription or an installment product. Clicking on the "Add Recurring" button will add a new row for you to choose from the list of available Recurring Profiles. You will need to create a Recurring Profile beforehand to assign it to the product. The fields are explained below.

Recurring Profile Select from the list of available recurring profiles already created in your store.
Customer Group Choose the customer group this recurring profile should be applied to.
